title=[B41/B42] Mechanical Dial Lock
description=[h1] Mechanical Dial Lock [/h1]
description=
description=This mod introduces functional safes with old-school mechanical dial locks. It's designed as a [b]framework[/b] mod, allowing other creators to expand it by creating addons with new safe designs.
description=
description=[h2]For Build 41:[/h2]
description=To build the safes included in this mod, you'll need the [b]Building Menu[/b] mod. This mod is required to access and build safes in your game.
description=[i]Note: Without the Building Menu mod, will not have access to the safes![/i]
description=
description=[h2]For Build 42:[/h2]
description=Safes are integrated into the vanilla Build Menu, no additional mods required.
description=
description=[u]Unlock Requirements:[/u]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Large Safes:[/b] Learned automatically at [b]Metalworking Level 8[/b].
description=[*][b]Small Safes:[/b] Learned automatically at [b]Metalworking Level 6[/b].
description=[/list]
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=[h2]Features:[/h2]
description=[b]1. Animated Sprites:[/b] The safes visually change to indicate their status (open/closed).
description=[b]2. User-Friendly Interface:[/b] Clear, step-by-step instructions for the mechanical lock make it easy to use.
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=
description=[h1] Frequently Asked Question: [/h1]
description=

description=Yes! This mod has been tested extensively and works perfectly in B41 MP servers.
description=
description=[h2] Does it work on existing saves? [/h2]
description=Yes! You can enable it mid-save without any issues.
description=
description=[h2] Does it work for all game versions? [/h2]
description=It works with the latest game version ([b]v41.78.16[/b] and [b]v42.x[/b]).
description=
